Pancakes

Light, fluffy, and golden pancakes perfect for breakfast or brunch.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter
  • Butter or oil for cooking

Steps

  1. In a bowl, whisk together flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  2. In another bowl, mix buttermilk, egg, and melted butter.
  3. Combine wet and dry ingredients, stirring until just mixed (donâ€™t overmix).
  4. Heat a non-stick pan over medium heat and grease lightly with butter or oil.
  5. Pour batter onto the pan and cook until bubbles form on the surface. Flip and cook until golden.
  6. Serve warm with syrup, butter, or toppings of your choice.
