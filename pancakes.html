Pancakes

Light, fluffy, and golden pancakes perfect for breakfast or brunch.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter
  • Butter or oil for cooking
  • hello

Steps

  1. In another bowl, mix buttermilk, egg, and melted butter.
  2. Serve warm with syrup, butter, or toppings of your choice.
