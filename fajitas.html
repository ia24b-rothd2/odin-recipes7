Fajitas

Delicious and smoky fajitas with marinated chicken, colorful peppers, and onions served in warm tortillas.

Ingredients

  • 2 chicken breasts, sliced into strips
  • 2 bell peppers (red and yellow), sliced
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Flour tortillas
  • Optional toppings: sour cream, guacamole, salsa, shredded cheese

Steps

  1. In a bowl, mix olive oil, chili powder, paprika, cumin, garlic powder, salt, and pepper to make the marinade.
  2. Add the chicken strips to the marinade and coat evenly. Let it rest for 15-20 minutes.
  3. Heat a large skillet or grill pan over medium-high heat. Cook the chicken until golden and fully cooked.
  4. Add the sliced peppers and onions to the pan. Stir-fry until slightly charred and tender.
  5. Warm the tortillas in a dry skillet or microwave.
  6. Assemble the fajitas by placing chicken and veggies on a tortilla. Add toppings as desired.
  7. Fold and enjoy warm!
